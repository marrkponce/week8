Mark Ponce

Mark Ponce

In 2010, Mark Ponce purchased his very first
longboard with birthday money he had received.


It wasn't until 2017 he began to
challenge himself to develope his craft.

In 2018, Mark moved to Los Angeles, California to connect
with other local riders. Him and a couple of friends started to create
content and push for the scene to grow.


Mark has been featured in promotional videos for Loaded Boards,
Orangatang Wheels, and Paris Truck Co. He has competed in
in SYCLD 2019, Paris 360, and SYCLD 2021. He received 1st place
for best G-Turn competition. Mark has experience teaching,
coaching, and mentoring longboard dancing and freestyle.
To this day, he is pushing for community growth.
